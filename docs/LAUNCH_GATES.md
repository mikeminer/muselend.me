# Mainnet launch gates

`MAINNET_ENABLED=false` remains mandatory until all boxes are independently evidenced.

- [ ] Independent external audit; every critical/high finding resolved.
- [ ] Accounting and economic-attack reviews.
- [ ] Italy/EU and target-market legal review, including MiCA/CASP, AML/sanctions,
      stablecoin/e-money, privacy, cookies and approved terms.
- [ ] Safe multisig, timelock and separate pause guardian configured and rehearsed.
- [ ] Monitoring, incident contacts, tested runbook and bug-bounty plan.
- [ ] Initial caps, fees, risk tiers and supported markets formally approved.
- [ ] Verified mainnet bytecode, addresses, deployment commit and audit commit.
- [ ] Jurisdiction/KYC/geofencing policy decision; feature adapters remain disabled pending it.

Mainnet deployment additionally requires the owner's exact approval phrase from the
product brief after full deployment data and maximum gas funding are shown.
